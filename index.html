Eligibilty Creteria

The competition is open for students of the age of 8 years (class 3) and above across all education boards.

The competition will be in 4 Stages in 2 rounds. The Stage 1 is Learning Stage to level the playing field. in Round 1 Unmask the hidden Skills and in Round 2 Mentoring students to Unleash Creativity

Stage 2 is Problem-Solving: Round 3 will be a Zonal level Creativity Challenge and in the Final round Zonal winners will compete at the National level

For a School to register school should appoint a Teacher coordinator (Science/Computers) for STEM Olympiad to coordinate. Maximum possible representation at a national level should be done.

The medium of instruction will be in English.
